# Meeple Meet  

**Play. Connect. Remember.**  

Board games are meant to bring people together, but keeping track of your collection, organizing sessions, and finding others to play with isn’t always easy. Meeple Meet is here to change that.  

It’s more than just a catalog for your games — it’s a place to store memories, share experiences, and connect with a wider community of players. Whether you’re documenting your latest playthrough, planning a game night, or looking for nearby shops and events, everything you need lives in one app.  

## Core Features  

- **Personal Game Management**: Track your board games, add notes, log sessions, and save photos to create a living history of your hobby.  
- **Offline Mode**: Update your collection or prepare a game night even while offline — your data syncs automatically when you’re back online.  
- **Community Interaction**: Chat with other players, start discussions about specific games, or create groups to stay in touch.  
- **Event Organization**: Set up events with time, place, and player info so everyone knows what to expect. Friends can join with a tap.  
- **Shop Integration**: Local stores can list available tables, let players reserve spots, and even run polls to decide which games get played.  
- **Sensor use**:  
  - **GPS** helps you find players, events, and shops nearby.  
  - **Camera** lets you capture memories or add new games (even custom ones) to the database.  
- **Designed for Everyone**:  
  - **Players** can grow their collections, meet new people, and relive favorite sessions.  
  - **Shops** get a way to connect with local gamers and promote their services.  
  - **Groups** keep everything in one place — chat, planning, and shared memories.
